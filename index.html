Kazi Nazrul Islam
The national poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet
Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangaldesh who is especially recognized as s Bidrohi Kobi (rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet muicisian and philosopher of Bangladesh.The famous poet,nazrul's writings deal with the revolution of that period of the british government. his revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of bangladesh. he has raised his voice against any kinds of fascism and oppression and got popular swifty around the indian subcontinent


Work
nazrul's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity and revolution. he opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism, including religious caste-based and gender- based. throughout his career. nazrul wrote short stories, novels and essays but is bet known for his songs and poems. he pioneered new music forms such as begali ghazals. nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramophone records.), collectively known as nazrul geeti.

"Love has no meaning or amount." - kazi Nazrul Islam
